About Us

Coding club is one of the many technical clubs of Central institute of Technology, Kokrajhar. Coding club aims to create an environment in the institute where everyone can learn and enjoy along with all the enthusiasts and grow the coding community.


Coding club has been running since 2021 under the leadership of various Leaders and faculties that are the driving force of the club.
